Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 20 paid leaves per calendar year.
Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days.

Sick Leave:
Employees may avail up to 10 sick leaves per year.
A medical certificate is required if sick leave exceeds 2 consecutive days.

Casual Leave:
Casual leave can be used for personal reasons with prior manager approval.

All leave requests must be submitted through the internal HR portal.
